Clock Preferences

Use the Clock Preferences to specify whether to display the &browser.name.prefix;TM &browser.name.suffix; clock, and to specify the clock's format. Access these preferences by choosing Edit->General Preferences. Then select Clock from the options on the left side of the General Preferences dialog box.

Choose Show Date, Show Time, or both if you'd like to display a clock in &browser.name;. Deselect both Show Date and Show Time if you don't want to display the clock at all.

The clock location is one of the following:

  • If the Status Bar is displayed, the clock is to the right of the message area. By default, the Status Bar is displayed at the bottom of the browser.

  • If you choose not to display the Status Bar, the clock is shown at the bottom right corner of the browser.

You can change the format of your clock by selecting a date format, a time format, or both from the scrolling lists of options. The date format is used only if the Show Date option is selected, and the time format is used only if the Show Time option is selected. If both options are selected, the date is displayed before the time.

The example clock located at the bottom right corner of the Clock Preferences page shows you exactly what the clock will look like with the settings you have selected. The format options provided in the scrolling lists are relevant to the locale in which you are running &browser.name;.
